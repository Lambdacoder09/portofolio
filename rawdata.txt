TherAssist is an AI-assisted tool designed to support therapists by providing an intelligent, context-aware assistant during patient sessions. It helps manage patient information, offers conversation suggestions, and can even digitize handwritten notes.

The application is built with a focus on a clean, cozy, and intuitive user experience that works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices.

‚ú® Features
Patient Management: Easily add new patients and switch between their session contexts from a responsive sidebar.
AI-Powered Sessions: Engage in a conversation where an AI assistant provides supportive and empathetic responses based on the dialogue history.
Persistent Patient Memory: Each patient has a dedicated "memory" file. This file stores crucial context, history, and notes, which are injected into the AI's prompts to ensure personalized and relevant interactions.
Memory Editing: View and update a patient's memory file on-the-fly to keep the AI's knowledge base current.
Scan Handwritten Notes: Upload a photo of handwritten notes. The AI will automatically transcribe the text and append it to the patient's memory file, seamlessly integrating physical notes into the digital context.
AI Suggestions: Receive real-time suggestions from the AI on what to say or ask next, helping to guide the conversation effectively.
Conversation Control: Clear the current session's conversation history for a fresh start.
Responsive & Aesthetic Design: A cozy, warm, and fully responsive interface ensures a seamless experience whether you're on a desktop or a mobile device.
